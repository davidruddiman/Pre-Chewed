Why Pre-Chewed?
💰
Fewer tokens, lower cost
⌄
PDFs, Word docs, PowerPoint decks, and spreadsheets carry fonts, images, layout data, and encoding overhead that AI models process before reading a single word. Stripping it all out means less wasted context window and a sharper, faster response from Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Microsoft Copilot, Perplexity, and every other AI tool you use.
🔌
Works where raw files don't
⌄
Many AI tools, automation workflows, and APIs don't accept PDFs or Office files, or handle them poorly. Plain text works everywhere. Paste it directly into Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Microsoft Copilot, Perplexity, Grok, Mistral, or any other prompt, tool, or pipeline.
🔒
Private up to your clipboard
⌄
Pre-Chewed runs entirely in your browser. Your file never touches a server. No upload, no account, no cost. Once you paste the output into Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Microsoft Copilot, Perplexity, Grok, or any other AI tool, that platform's own privacy policy applies from that point on.
Pre-Chewed removes images and layout data, but the text remains subject to its original copyright. If your document contains proprietary or third-party content, it's your responsibility to ensure you have the right to share it with whichever AI tool you use.
✍️
Prep once, use anywhere
⌄
Convert your PDF, Word doc, PowerPoint deck, or spreadsheet once and paste the clean output into Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Microsoft Copilot, Perplexity, Grok, or any other AI tool. Headings, bullet points, tables, and structure are preserved so the model understands your document the same way you do.
